About Mithai Magic

Founded in 2010, Mithai Magic has established itself as a beloved neighborhood sweet shop committed to creating authentic Indian sweets using traditional methods and the finest ingredients.

Our team of talented halwais (sweet makers) brings together diverse expertise from various regions of India to deliver exceptional treats that delight the senses.
